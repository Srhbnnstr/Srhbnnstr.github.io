ABOUT ME

My whole life, I’ve accepted the challenge of building and creating anything I can get my hands on—tinkering on my motorcycle, building a PC, or creating concept pixel art inspired by my love of video games. After working in the hair industry, I sought to continue the excitement and passion I felt when I started teaching myself coding and design. My experience at DESIGNATION helped build this into a career. Today, I seek opportunities to practice UX, with hefty doses of front-end development and UI design. And I’m all about craft nights, dogs, nachos, and seeing the world. Contact me to work together or to chat about shared interests.
