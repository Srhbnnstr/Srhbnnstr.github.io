ABOUT ME

My whole life, I’ve accepted the challenge of building and creating anything I can get my hands on—tinkering on my motorcycle, building a PC, or creating concept pixel art inspired by my love of video games. After working in the hair industry, I sought to continue the excitement and passion I felt when I started teaching myself coding and design. Since then, I have spent my time devoting myself to becoming the agile UX designer I am today. My experiences at General Assembly and DESIGNATION helped me build this into a career. Providing people with delightful digital experiences that fulfill their goals and needs is my passion, I love making the daily lives of the community better in this way. In my free time, you can find me working to better local websites, learning something new or getting inspired by other creative people around me.
